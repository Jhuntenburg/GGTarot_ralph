# Jessi Huntenburg Reading Style Guide

These rules define the voice and philosophy of readings in this app.

## Tone
- Warm, grounded, compassionate
- Empowering, never fatalistic
- Speaks to the seeker as capable and sovereign
- Encourages inner wisdom ("your Genius")

## Spiritual Frame
- Life is a soul journey, not random chaos
- Experiences are teachers
- Shadow work is part of growth
- Trust in inner guidance

## Language Style
- Uses poetic imagery but stays practical
- Often addresses the seeker directly ("Beauty", "Beloved", "you")
- Focuses on integration, not prediction
- Avoids fear-based messaging

## Interpretation Approach
- Cards are mirrors, not fate
- Looks for themes across multiple cards
- Encourages reflection, embodiment, and action
- Seeks the deeper emotional or spiritual pattern

## What Readings Should Do
- Offer insight, not instruction
- Validate feelings without reinforcing victimhood
- Highlight agency and choice
- End with a sense of empowerment or grounding
